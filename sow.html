Section 1

Project Overview

Jarvis Daily ("Service Provider", a unit of Tranquil Veda Private Limited, GSTIN 27AAHCT6956C1Z3, SAC 998314) will deploy a private, production-ready instance of the Brokerage Command Centre for Saffron Realty Channel Partners ("Client", Pune and Mumbai), configured around Saffron Realty's lead-source mix, channel-partner roster, builder relationships and brand. Through this engagement Saffron Realty will:

  • Respond to every inbound lead from MagicBricks, 99acres, Housing.com, Meta lead-ads, and WhatsApp inbound within 60 seconds in the agent's voice, with budget, timeline and locality captured in the same turn
  • Route every qualified lead to the right channel-partner agent based on price band, micro-market and live capacity
  • Assemble premium-project listing brochures as cinematic mini-websites (RERA registration number auto-stamped, floor plans, price grid, virtual walkthrough link) in under 5 minutes
  • Run drip nurture flows that bring back leads gone cold and trigger missed-follow-up alerts before deals slip past the 7-day re-engagement window
  • Track channel-partner commissions with a transparent audit trail of every touchpoint, retiring the quarterly Excel argument
  • Capture post-site-visit intent signals via a Site-Visit Voice Drop, auto-tagged to the lead and lookable by the closing manager
  • Retain RERA disclosure compliance on every outbound brokerage communication, with the project's MahaRERA or RERA Pune registration number auto-attached

The system is hosted on managed infrastructure procured and maintained by Jarvis Daily on Saffron Realty's behalf. Build duration is 8 calendar weeks from kickoff (5 build + 3 hypercare), followed by 30 days of daily-check-in hypercare and a 12-month support retainer commencing per Section 5.5.

5.5 Retainer Commencement

The monthly retainer commences on the earlier of (a) the calendar month in which go-live or final handover is achieved, or (b) the calendar month falling 12 weeks after the kickoff date. This dual-trigger is standard for managed-platform engagements and gives both sides a clear, calendar-anchored start to the steady-state support cycle. Where joint readiness checkpoints, configuration handovers, or sign-off cycles run past date (b), any continuing build work is carried out under the Change Request Process in Section 11 and date (b) remains the retainer-start date. Section 7

Compliance Representation

Saffron Realty is responsible for the legality, taxation correctness and brokerage standards of every communication sent through the system. Jarvis Daily is a software vendor and does not assume regulatory liability for the brokerage advice or transactions contained within client interactions.

  • RERA disclosure. The Brokerage Command Centre maintains an immutable RERA-disclosure audit log of every outbound communication that references a specific project, retained for 5 years. Saffron Realty's MahaRERA and RERA Pune broker registration numbers, along with each builder project's RERA registration, are recorded as configuration and auto-stamped on the relevant messages and brochures.
  • DPDP Act 2023. Inbound leads are treated as Data Principals. Consent text on the AI Lead Qualifier flow is reviewed against the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. The system supports access, correction and erasure requests via the admin panel.
  • GST and SAC. Jarvis Daily invoices Saffron Realty under SAC 998314 (IT consulting and software services). Saffron Realty's CA remains the final authority on tax classification.
  • IT Act 2000. Digital signatures collected through the platform are legally valid under Section 5 of the Information Technology Act 2000 and its 2008 amendment.

Section 11

Termination, Change Requests and Force Majeure

11.1 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate this SOW with thirty (30) days' written notice. On termination, Saffron Realty shall pay for all work completed up to the date of termination and Jarvis Daily shall deliver a final data export within 7 business days.

11.2 Change Request Process

Any out-of-scope work is documented in a written change request, sized in engineering hours by Jarvis Daily, and signed off by Saffron Realty's nominated authoriser before work begins. Change requests are billed at ₹3,500 per engineering hour + 18% GST.

11.3 Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by acts of God, war, terrorism, civil unrest, government action, pandemic, or other events beyond reasonable control. Where Force Majeure extends the build, the retainer-commencement backstop date in Section 5.5 moves on a day-for-day basis.
