What happens if nothing changes.

Two costs compound. First, every ghosted lead is a commission you never see - on premium ticket sizes those add up to roughly ₹40 lakh to ₹60 lakh a year at conservative conversion math. Second, the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 and Maharashtra Rule 35 require every brokerage communication referencing a project to carry that project's MahaRERA / RERA Pune registration number, and brokers must retain a 5-year compliance log. Today you do this manually, on best effort. A single complaint to MahaRERA over a missed disclosure is the kind of thing that costs more in defence time than the lead would have earned. The system below stamps RERA on every outbound message automatically and keeps the 5-year log without anyone having to remember.

The five risks we flag before you do.

Every engagement at this scale has predictable failure modes. Naming them up front is how we keep the conversation honest.

Channel-partner agents keep replying from personal WhatsApp and never adopt the team surface.
Mitigation. Two safety nets. First, start at Lead Engine (₹2,25,000) instead of full Command Centre - the agent-facing surface stays WhatsApp on their own phone, no new app. Second, if you take the full Command Centre, the week-6 adoption KPI is in the SOW: fewer than 40% of inbound handled through the system triggers a 30% refund of unused Phase C fees and a pivot back to the Lead Engine SKU.
A bot misquotes a project price or a builder commitment to a real buyer.
Mitigation. Every reply that quotes a price, a payment plan or a builder commitment beyond the public listing data is gated on one-tap agent approval. Cold-opener replies are fully automated. The line between the two is locked in the SOW. Confidence threshold below 80% routes to a human - no auto-send.
Saffron Realty WhatsApp number gets flagged or rate-limited by Meta.
Mitigation. Meta WhatsApp Business Cloud API with a verified Saffron Realty number, throttled outbound, business-hours sends only, warmup ramp for the new number, conversation-template review. If the number is ever flagged, we activate a replacement SIM and re-configure the system at no additional cost during the active retainer period.
A RERA disclosure is missed on an outbound message and triggers a complaint.
Mitigation. Hard gate at the send queue. Any outbound message that references a project name or project price without the project's MahaRERA / RERA Pune number on file is held in a manual-review queue, not sent. The 5-year compliance log writes itself per Maharashtra Rule 35.
Monthly fee starts to feel like wasted spend in month 4.
Mitigation. Prepaid SKU (₹9,20,000) bundles year one monthly into setup, so there is no month 4 invoice. Year two is framed as Compliance, Hosting and Continuity - the 5-year RERA audit trail is the anchor value, not "maintenance".
